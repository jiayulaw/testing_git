About

The Nott-A-Thon: Lovelace is a programming competition organized by the Institution of Engineering and Technology UNM Student Chapter. We invited teams of foundation and undergraduate students in Malaysia, as an initiative to promote competitive programming among students. We provide an environment for students to level up their skills and win cash prizes. E-certificate and s-CPD point(only applicable to Nottingham students) will be awarded after the contest.
